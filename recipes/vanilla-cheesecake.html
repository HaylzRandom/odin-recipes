Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Digestive Biscuits
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Double Cream

Steps

  1. Crush digestive biscuits and melt butter
  2. Mix biscuit and butter together and create base in cake pan
  3. Stick cake pan in fridge to firm up
  4. Mix cream cheese and double cream together
  5. Place cheesecake mixture on top of biscuit base
  6. Store in fridge until firm
